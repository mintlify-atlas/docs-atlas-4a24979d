title: Finding Healthcare Facilities
description: Discover how to search and locate healthcare services near you
---

SaludMap helps you find healthcare facilities like hospitals, clinics, doctors, and veterinary services in your area.

## Getting Your Location

Before you can find nearby facilities, SaludMap needs to know your location.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant location permissions">
    When you first open SaludMap, your browser will ask for permission to access your location. Click "Allow" to enable location services.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for location detection">
    The map will automatically center on your current position. You'll see a marker indicating your location.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update location if needed">
    If your location changes or isn't accurate, click the "Mi Ubicación" (My Location) button to recenter the map on your current position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Filters

Filter healthcare facilities by type to find exactly what you need.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the filters modal">
    Click the filters button (usually a funnel icon) to open the facility type selector.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select facility types">
    Choose from available facility types:
    - **Hospital** - Full-service hospitals
    - **Clínica** - Clinics and medical centers
    - **Médico** - Doctor's offices
    - **Veterinaria** - Veterinary services
    - And more healthcare service types
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use the 'All' toggle">
    Click the "Todos" (All) button to quickly select or deselect all facility types at once.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply filters">
    The map will automatically update to show only the selected facility types. Markers will appear for nearby establishments matching your filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Facility Information

Once facilities appear on the map, you can view detailed information about each one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a facility marker">
    Click on any marker or search result to view the facility details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review the information panel">
    The facility information panel displays:
    - **Name** - Establishment name
    - **Address** - Street address or coordinates
    - **Phone** - Contact number (if available)
    - **Opening Hours** - Business hours (if available)
    - **Tags** - Additional service information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View reviews and ratings">
    Scroll down in the panel to see user reviews and average star ratings for the facility.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Searching for Facilities

Use the search feature to find specific healthcare services:

1. Enter keywords in the search box (e.g., "hospital", "pediatra", "emergencia")
2. The map will filter results based on your search terms
3. Click on results to view detailed information

## Map Controls

- **Zoom In/Out** - Use the + and - buttons or scroll wheel to adjust zoom level
- **Pan** - Click and drag the map to explore different areas
- **Recenter** - Click the location button to return to your current position
- **Offline Mode** - Download an area for offline access using the download button

## Understanding Markers

Different markers represent different facility types:
- Color-coded pins indicate the type of healthcare service
- Cluster markers group nearby facilities at lower zoom levels
- Click any marker to expand details

<Tip>
  For the most accurate results, make sure your device's GPS is enabled and you have a stable internet connection. You can also download areas for offline use if you'll be in an area with limited connectivity.
</Tip>
