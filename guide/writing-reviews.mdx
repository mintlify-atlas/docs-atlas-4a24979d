SaludMap's review system allows you to share your experiences and help others make informed decisions about healthcare services.

## Prerequisites

To write a review, you must:
- Have an account and be logged in
- Have completed an appointment (turno) at the facility
- The appointment must be in the past (you can only review completed visits)

## Writing a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a facility">
    Click on a healthcare facility marker on the map to open its information panel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open the review form">
    Scroll to the reviews section and click the "Dejar una reseña" (Leave a review) button. You must be logged in to see this option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select the appointment">
    If you have multiple past appointments at this facility, select which visit you're reviewing from the dropdown menu.
    
    The dropdown shows:
    - Appointment date
    - Time (if available)
    - Professional name (if available)
    
    If you only have one eligible appointment, it will be automatically selected.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate your experience">
    Click on the stars to rate the facility from 1 to 5:
    - ★☆☆☆☆ (1 star) - Poor experience
    - ★★☆☆☆ (2 stars) - Below expectations
    - ★★★☆☆ (3 stars) - Average
    - ★★★★☆ (4 stars) - Good experience
    - ★★★★★ (5 stars) - Excellent experience
    
    Hover over the stars to preview your rating.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write your comment">
    In the text area, share your experience with the facility. Your comment should:
    - Be at least 10 characters long
    - Be no more than 1,000 characters
    - Be respectful and constructive
    - Focus on your actual experience
    
    Good things to include:
    - Quality of care received
    - Wait times
    - Staff professionalism
    - Facility cleanliness
    - Overall impression
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your review">
    Click the "Publicar reseña" (Publish review) button to submit. You'll see a success message when your review is posted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review Guidelines

### Character Limits
- **Minimum**: 10 characters
- **Maximum**: 1,000 characters
- A character counter shows your progress as you type

### What Makes a Good Review

A helpful review typically includes:
- **Specific details** about your visit
- **Balanced perspective** (pros and cons if applicable)
- **Relevant information** that helps others
- **Respectful language** even for negative experiences

### Review Restrictions

You can only review:
- Facilities where you've had appointments
- Appointments that have already occurred (past dates)
- Each appointment can be reviewed once

## Viewing Reviews

### Reading Other Reviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open facility details">
    Click on any facility marker to view its information panel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to reviews section">
    Scroll down to the "Reseñas" (Reviews) section or click the reviews tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View review summary">
    At the top, you'll see:
    - Average star rating (e.g., 4.5)
    - Total number of reviews
    - Star rating visualization
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read individual reviews">
    Scroll through the list to read reviews from other users. Each review shows:
    - Reviewer's name (first name and last name initials)
    - Profile avatar (initials)
    - Review date
    - Star rating
    - Written comment
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Review Displays

### User Information
- Reviews show the reviewer's first name and last initial
- An avatar displays the user's initials
- Review creation date is shown in a readable format (e.g., "15 de marzo, 2026")

### Star Ratings
- Individual review ratings appear next to each review
- The average rating is prominently displayed at the top
- Empty facilities show: "📝 Aún no hay reseñas para este establecimiento" (No reviews yet)

## After Posting a Review

Once you submit a review:
- It appears immediately in the facility's review list
- Other users can read your review
- The facility's average rating updates automatically
- You'll receive a success notification

## Eligibility Notifications

**If you have no eligible appointments:**

You'll see: "⚠️ No tienes turnos disponibles para reseñar en este establecimiento"

**Subtitle:** "Solo puedes dejar reseñas para turnos que ya hayan pasado"

This means:
- You haven't booked any appointments at this facility, OR
- Your appointments haven't occurred yet

## Review Validation

The system validates:
- ✓ User is logged in
- ✓ An appointment is selected
- ✓ A star rating is chosen (1-5)
- ✓ Comment meets minimum length (10 characters)
- ✓ Comment doesn't exceed maximum length (1,000 characters)

<Note>
  Reviews are tied to specific appointments to ensure authenticity. You must have actually visited the facility to leave a review.
</Note>

## Tips for Writing Helpful Reviews

<Tip>
  Focus on actionable feedback that helps both potential patients and the healthcare facility improve. Be specific about what made your experience positive or negative.
</Tip>

**Examples of helpful details:**
- "The wait time was minimal, only about 10 minutes"
- "Dr. Smith explained everything clearly and answered all my questions"
- "The facility was very clean and well-maintained"
- "Staff was friendly and the scheduling process was easy"

**Avoid:**
- Personal attacks or offensive language
- Sharing private medical information
- Unverified claims or rumors
- Duplicate reviews for the same visit
