title: Managing Saved Locations
description: Save and organize your favorite places and frequently visited healthcare facilities

SaludMap allows you to save specific map locations for quick access later. This is useful for remembering healthcare facilities, home addresses, work locations, or any place you visit frequently.

## Saving a Location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Position the map">
    Navigate the map to the location you want to save. You can:
    - Pan by dragging the map
    - Zoom to the desired detail level
    - Use your current location or search for a specific place
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open the save location modal">
    Click the "Guardar Ubicación" (Save Location) button. This is typically found in the map controls.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter a name">
    In the modal, provide a descriptive name for the location (required):
    - Maximum 50 characters
    - Examples: "Mi Casa", "Clínica Favorita", "Trabajo", "Hospital San Juan"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add a description (optional)">
    Optionally, add a description with more details:
    - Maximum 200 characters
    - Examples: "Cerca del supermercado", "Entrada por calle lateral", "Consulta de cardiología"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review coordinates">
    The modal displays the exact coordinates being saved:
    - Latitude and longitude (up to 6 decimal places)
    - GPS accuracy (if available)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the location">
    Click the "Guardar Ubicación" (Save Location) button. You'll see a success message: "Ubicación guardada exitosamente" (Location saved successfully).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Saved Locations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the locations list">
    Click the "Ver Ubicaciones" (View Locations) button to open your saved locations list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse your locations">
    The list displays all your saved locations with:
    - **Name** - The title you gave the location
    - **Description** - Any additional notes (if provided)
    - **Coordinates** - Exact latitude and longitude
    - **Save Date** - When the location was saved (e.g., "9 mar. 2026, 15:30")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use the action buttons">
    Each location has two buttons:
    - **📍 Go** - Navigate to this location on the map
    - **🗑️ Delete** - Remove the location from your saved list
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigating to a Saved Location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your locations list">
    Click "Ver Ubicaciones" to view all saved locations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a location">
    Find the location you want to visit in the list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the Go button">
    Click the 📍 button next to the location. The map will:
    - Automatically center on the saved coordinates
    - Close the locations list modal
    - Display any nearby facilities or markers
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Saved Location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your locations list">
    Access your saved locations by clicking "Ver Ubicaciones".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find the location to delete">
    Locate the location you want to remove from your list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the delete button">
    Click the 🗑️ (trash) button next to the location.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    A confirmation toast will appear asking: "¿Eliminar '[Location Name]'?"
    
    Click:
    - **Eliminar** (Delete) - to permanently remove the location
    - **Cancelar** (Cancel) - to keep the location
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Deletion confirmed">
    If you confirm, you'll see: "Ubicación eliminada" (Location deleted) and the location will be removed from your list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Location Information Display

### Coordinate Format
Coordinates are displayed with 6 decimal places for precision:
- Example: `-34.603722, -58.381592`
- This provides accuracy within a few meters

### Timestamp Format
Save dates use the Spanish locale format:
- Short month name (ene., feb., mar., etc.)
- Day and year
- 24-hour time format
- Example: "9 mar. 2026, 15:30"

### Location Accuracy
If available, the GPS accuracy is shown when saving:
- Displayed in meters (e.g., "~15m")
- Indicates how precise the coordinate reading is
- Lower numbers mean higher accuracy

## Empty State

If you haven't saved any locations yet, you'll see:

**Icon**: 📍

**Message**: "No hay ubicaciones guardadas"

**Subtitle**: "Usa el botón 'Guardar Ubicación' en el mapa para agregar tus lugares favoritos"

## Storage and Persistence

Saved locations are:
- Stored in your browser's local database (IndexedDB)
- Persistent across sessions
- Available offline once saved
- Specific to your device and browser

<Warning>
  Clearing your browser data or cache may delete your saved locations. Consider exporting important locations if available, or keep a backup list.
</Warning>

- Keep track of pharmacy locations

- Work location

### Trip Planning

## Validation and Errors

### Required Fields
- **Name**: Must be filled in (at least 1 character after trimming whitespace)
- **Current Location**: Must be available (map must have valid coordinates)

### Error Messages

You might see:
- **"map.locationNameRequired"** - You must enter a name
- **"map.noLocationAvailable"** - No valid coordinates to save
- **"map.errorSaving"** - Failed to save to local database
- **"map.errorLoading"** - Failed to load saved locations
- **"map.errorDeleting"** - Failed to delete a location
- **"map.errorGoingTo"** - Failed to navigate to location

<Tip>
  Give your saved locations descriptive names that help you remember why you saved them. Instead of "Location 1", use names like "Dr. Martinez Office" or "Emergency Clinic Downtown".
</Tip>

## Managing Multiple Locations

There's no limit to how many locations you can save:
- They're displayed in the order they were saved
- Use descriptive names to keep them organized
- Delete locations you no longer need to keep the list manageable
- The list is scrollable if you have many saved locations
