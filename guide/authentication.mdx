---
title: User Authentication
description: Learn how to create an account and sign in to SaludMap
---

SaludMap provides user authentication to enable personalized features like booking appointments, writing reviews, and managing saved locations.

## Creating an Account

To access protected features in SaludMap, you'll need to create an account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trigger the registration modal">
    When you try to access a protected feature (like writing a review or booking an appointment), the authentication modal will automatically appear. You can also click the "Register" or "Login" button in the navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Switch to registration mode">
    If the login form is showing, click the "Registrarse" (Register) link at the bottom of the modal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in your information">
    Enter the following required information:
    - **First Name** (Nombre)
    - **Last Name** (Apellido)
    - **Email** - Must be a valid email address
    - **Password** (Contraseña)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click the "Registrarse" button to create your account. If registration is successful, you'll be automatically logged in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing In

If you already have an account, you can sign in to access your personalized features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login modal">
    Click the "Login" button or try to access a protected feature to trigger the authentication modal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Provide your:
    - **Email** - The email address you registered with
    - **Password** - Your account password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click the "Ingresar" (Login) button to sign in. Your session will be saved locally for future visits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session Management

Once logged in:
- Your user information is stored locally in your browser
- A JWT token is saved for authenticating API requests
- You remain logged in across browser sessions until you explicitly log out

## Signing Out

To sign out of your account:
1. Click on your user profile or account menu
2. Select "Logout" or "Cerrar sesión"
3. Your session data will be cleared from local storage

## Password Requirements

- Passwords must be entered twice during registration to confirm
- Use a strong password to protect your account
- Password reset functionality may be available through the backend API

## Protected Features

The following features require authentication:
- Writing reviews for healthcare facilities
- Booking appointments (turnos)
- Viewing your appointment history
- Saving personalized preferences

<Note>
  If you encounter authentication errors, make sure all form fields are filled out correctly and your email follows the proper format (e.g., user@example.com).
</Note>
