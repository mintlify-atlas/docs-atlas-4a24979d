---
title: Booking Appointments
description: Learn how to request and manage appointments (turnos) at healthcare facilities
---

SaludMap allows you to book appointments (called "turnos") at healthcare facilities directly through the application.

## Prerequisites

Before booking an appointment, make sure you:
- Have created an account and are logged in
- Have selected a healthcare facility from the map
- Have your email address ready

## Booking an Appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a facility">
    Click on a healthcare facility marker on the map to view its details. Look for facilities that offer appointment booking.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open the appointment modal">
    In the facility information panel, click the "Solicitar Turno" (Request Appointment) button. If you're not logged in, you'll be prompted to sign in first.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a specialty">
    Select the medical specialty you need from the dropdown list. Each specialty displays its available hours (e.g., "Lunes a Viernes: 09:00 - 17:00").
    
    Available specialties vary by facility and may include:
    - General Medicine (Medicina General)
    - Pediatrics (Pediatría)
    - Cardiology (Cardiología)
    - Dentistry (Odontología)
    - And more
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a date">
    Use the visual calendar to choose an appointment date:
    - **Green days** - Available for appointments
    - **Gray days** - Not available or past dates
    - **Highlighted** - Today's date
    - **Selected** - Your chosen date
    
    Click on any available (green) date to select it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a time slot">
    After selecting a date, available time slots will appear in 15-minute intervals. Click on your preferred time slot to select it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add observations (optional)">
    In the "Observaciones" (Observations) field, you can add any relevant notes about your appointment, such as:
    - Specific symptoms or concerns
    - Reason for visit
    - Special requirements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Enter your email address in the footer field. This will be used for appointment confirmations and notifications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Click the "Confirmar Turno" (Confirm Appointment) button to submit your appointment request. You'll see a confirmation message if successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Appointment Availability

### Operating Hours
Each specialty has specific operating hours displayed in the format:
- "Lunes a Viernes: 09:00 - 17:00" (Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
- "Sábados: 09:00 - 13:00" (Saturdays: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

Only dates and times within these hours will be available for booking.

### Time Slots
- Appointments are available in 15-minute intervals
- Slots are generated based on the specialty's operating hours
- Already booked slots may not be visible

## Managing Your Appointments

To view and manage your booked appointments:

1. Navigate to "Mis Turnos" (My Appointments) in the menu
2. View your list of upcoming and past appointments
3. Each appointment shows:
   - Facility name and location
   - Specialty
   - Date and time
   - Status (confirmed, cancelled, etc.)

## Cancelling an Appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your appointments list">
    Open the "Mis Turnos" section to view your appointments.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select the appointment">
    Find the appointment you want to cancel in the list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm cancellation">
    Click the cancel button and confirm your decision. The appointment status will be updated to "cancelled".
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Please cancel appointments as early as possible to allow other patients to book that time slot. Check the facility's cancellation policy for any time restrictions.
</Warning>

## Appointment Notifications

You'll receive notifications about your appointments via:
- Email confirmations
- Status updates
- Reminder notifications (if enabled)

## Troubleshooting

**No available slots showing:**
- Try a different date within the operating hours
- Check if the specialty has limited availability
- Contact the facility directly for urgent appointments

**Can't select a date:**
- Ensure the date falls within the specialty's operating days
- Past dates and dates outside operating hours cannot be selected

**Booking fails:**
- Verify all required fields are filled (specialty, date, time, email)
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

<Tip>
  When booking from the specialty search feature, the specialty will be pre-selected for you, making the booking process faster.
</Tip>
