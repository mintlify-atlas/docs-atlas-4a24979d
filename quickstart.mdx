## Welcome to SaludMap

SaludMap is a healthcare facility locator that helps you find hospitals, clinics, medical centers, and veterinary services near you. This guide will walk you through the essential features to get you started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Application">
    Open SaludMap in your web browser. The application will load with an interactive map interface.
    
    <Note>
      SaludMap works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow Location Permissions">
    When prompted, click **Allow** to grant location permissions. This enables SaludMap to:
    - Show your current location on the map
    - Find nearby healthcare facilities
    - Provide accurate distance calculations
    
    <Info>
      You can manually search for locations if you prefer not to share your location.
    </Info>
    
    ### Getting Your Location
    
    Click the **location button** (📍) in the map controls to center the map on your current position. The map will automatically recenter as your location changes.
    
    <Tip>
      Use the **Stop button** to disable continuous location tracking and save battery.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for Healthcare Facilities">
    SaludMap offers multiple ways to find the care you need:
    
    ### Using Filters
    
    1. Click the **Filters button** to open the filter modal
    2. Select facility types:
       - Hospital
       - Clínica (Clinic)
       - Médico (Doctor)
       - Veterinaria (Veterinary)
       - And more...
    3. Click **Todos** (All) to quickly select or deselect all filters
    4. Apply your selections to see matching facilities on the map
    
    ### Searching Nearby
    
    The app automatically displays nearby facilities based on your current map view. As you pan and zoom the map, relevant facilities appear as markers.
    
    <Tip>
      Zoom in closer to see more detailed facility information and discover smaller clinics in your area.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Facility Details">
    Click on any facility marker to open detailed information:
    
    - **Name and Type**: Facility name and category
    - **Address**: Full street address with directions
    - **Phone Number**: Direct contact for inquiries
    - **Operating Hours**: Opening hours and current status (open/closed)
    - **Specialties**: Available medical specialties and services
    - **Reviews**: User ratings and comments
    - **Analytics**: Basic statistics and metrics (if available)
    
    <Info>
      The operating hours feature shows real-time status, so you'll know if a facility is currently open before you visit.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Book an Appointment">
    To reserve an appointment at a facility:
    
    ### Authentication Required
    
    If you haven't logged in yet, you'll see an authentication modal. Choose to:
    - **Sign In**: Use your existing account credentials
    - **Register**: Create a new account with your name, email, and password
    
    <CodeGroup>
    ```text Sign In
    Email: your.email@example.com
    Password: ••••••••
    ```
    
    ```text Register
    Name: Your Name
    Last Name: Your Last Name
    Email: your.email@example.com
    Password: ••••••••
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
    
    ### Request an Appointment
    
    Once authenticated:
    
    1. Open the facility details from the map
    2. Click **Request Appointment** (Solicitar Turno)
    3. Select:
       - **Specialty**: Choose from available medical specialties
       - **Date**: Pick your preferred appointment date
       - **Time**: Select an available time slot
    4. Confirm your request
    5. You'll receive feedback on your appointment status (pending/confirmed)
    
    <Warning>
      Appointment availability depends on the facility's schedule. Some facilities may require phone confirmation.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Additional Features

### Save Your Favorite Locations

Click **Save Location** to store the current map position with a custom name. Access your saved locations anytime from the saved locations list.

### Read and Write Reviews

After visiting a facility:
1. Open the facility details
2. Navigate to the reviews section
3. Add your rating (1-5 stars) and comment
4. Help others make informed healthcare decisions

<Note>
You must be logged in and have a completed appointment to write a review.
</Note>

### Customize Your Experience

#### Theme Selection
Click the **theme button** to toggle between light and dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

#### Language Options
Use the **language selector** to switch between supported languages. The entire interface updates instantly.

### Offline Access

SaludMap supports offline functionality:

1. Click the **Download Area** button
2. Select the region you want to preload
3. The app downloads map tiles and facility data
4. Access this information even without an internet connection

<Tip>
Download your home and work areas for reliable access anywhere, anytime.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Account

### View Your Appointments

Access your appointment history and upcoming visits from your user profile. Track:
- Pending appointments
- Confirmed visits
- Past appointments
- Appointment details and facility information

### User Preferences

All your settings sync across sessions:
- Theme preference (light/dark mode)
- Language selection
- Saved locations
- Map view preferences

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues or have questions:
- Check facility details for direct contact information
- Use the in-app feedback options
- Contact facility support directly for appointment-specific questions
